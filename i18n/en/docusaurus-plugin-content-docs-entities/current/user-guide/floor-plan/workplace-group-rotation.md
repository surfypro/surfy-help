# Group rotation of workstations on the floor plan

On a floor plan view (<LIV code="floor:map" />), in **edit mode**, you can rotate **several workstations at once** when they are selected together.

<CloudinaryAsset publicId="help/changelog/v3.5.15/workplace-group-rotation-en" kind="video" asGif width={640} gifFps={8} alt="Group rotation of workstations in plan edit mode" />

## Prerequisites

- Edit rights on the floor plan.
- **Edit mode** enabled (shortcut **e** or the equivalent option in the plan toolbar).

## Steps

1. Open the floor plan.
2. Turn on **edit mode**.
3. Select **at least two workstations**:
   - hold **Shift** and click each workstation, or
   - use the selection tool to include several workstations.
4. A **dashed frame** surrounds the selection. On the **top edge** of the frame, three actions are available (left to right):
   - move the selection;
   - duplicate the selection (if enabled for your organization);
   - **rotate the workstations together** (rotation icon).
5. Drag the rotation handle: all selected workstations rotate **around the centre of the selection**, keeping their relative layout.
6. Release the mouse button: the orientation is saved on each workstation.

## Single workstation rotation

When **one** workstation is selected, the rotation handle stays on that workstation (unchanged behaviour).

## Tip

- Hold **Shift** while rotating for a **free angle**.
- Without Shift, rotation snaps to **45°** steps.

## Limits (current version)

- Group rotation applies to **workstations** only (not a mixed selection of workstations and furniture items in one gesture).
- The building **3D** view is not included.
