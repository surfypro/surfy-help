# Getting Started Guide

Two prerequisites are required to use meeting space viewing and booking:

-   Configuring the "space typology group" for spaces offered for viewing/booking,
-   Assigning the email address of bookable spaces for meeting space booking.

## Configure viewable spaces

### Space typology group

Space typologies corresponding to viewable spaces must be grouped in the "Meeting Spaces" space typology group.<br />
The code MEETING must be associated with the "Meeting Spaces" space typology group.

From the left menu,

-   click on "Administration", "Typologies", "Space typologies", then "Space typology groups" to "List space typology groups"
-   click on the icon at the beginning of the "Meeting Spaces" space typology group row to "Modify space typology group Meeting Spaces"
-   enter "MEETING" in the "Code" field.

### Associate a space typology with a space typology group

Once the viewable space typology group has been set with the MEETING code, each viewable typology must be associated with this group.
For example: "meeting rooms", "meeting box", and "phone box" can be typologies associated with the "Meeting Spaces" space typology group.

To associate a space typology with a typology group, see the chapter [associate a space typology with a space typology group](/en/docs/tutorials/surfaces/room/roomtype#space-typology-groups).

Spaces included in the "Meeting Spaces" space typology group can be viewed by clicking the "View and navigate in meeting rooms" icon at the top left of the screen under the company logo.

### Configure equipment for viewable spaces

You can display the equipment of each meeting space via the object family containing the objects to display (audiovisual, service equipment, ...).

To configure viewable equipment, from the left menu,

-   click on "Furniture", "Typologies", "Object type family" to "List object type families"
-   click on the "3 dots" icon at the beginning of the row of the equipment family to view, then "See more options for object type family XXX", then "Modify object type family XXX"
-   enter "SERVICES" in the "Code" field
-   save the modifications
-   refresh the page (F5)

Equipment from the configured family present in the space is visible for each space when viewing meeting spaces.

## Configure bookable spaces

Once viewable spaces have been configured correctly, each viewable space can be declared "bookable".

Each bookable space must be associated with a booking email that characterizes the space.
This email is usually assigned by IT and must be entered in the card of the relevant space.

From the plan where the space is located,

-   click on the space to select it
-   click on the "3 dots" icon at the bottom of the space card to "See more options for space XXX", then on "Modify space XXX"
-   enter the space booking email address in "Email used for booking this space"
-   save the modifications

The space is then available for viewing/booking by clicking the "View and navigate in meeting rooms" icon under the company logo.
