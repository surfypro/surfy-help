# Getting started guide

This guide details the process for implementing parking space booking with Surfy.

## Prerequisites for using booking with Surfy

Surfy offers to digitize building plans to facilitate overall workplace environment management.<br />
To use Surfy's booking features, the company must have a Surfy platform.<br />

If the company is not yet using Surfy, it is necessary to contact our teams via our [contact page](https://www.surfy.pro/en-gb/contact).<br />
A dedicated platform will be assigned to you, a building must be digitized, and people must be created.<br />

If the company's plans are already digitized in Surfy, booking features are available immediately, after the company accepts the booking license with Surfy ([Contact us](https://www.surfy.pro/en-gb/contact)), and after [vehicle association](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/parkingbooking/admin#associate-vehicles-with-collaborators) and [building assignment](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/parkingbooking/admin#building-assignment-for-parking-booking) for parking booking have been set up for collaborators.

## Parking space booking features

Each authorized collaborator can use parking space booking features depending on:

-   the building to which the collaborator is assigned for parking space booking
-   the vehicle type associated with the collaborator (electric, hybrid, accessible parking, ...)

## Parking booking configuration settings

Parking booking configuration settings are accessible to users with an Administrator role in Surfy.

### Vehicle types

Vehicle types are pre-established by Surfy: internal combustion, electric, hybrid, accessible internal combustion, accessible electric, accessible hybrid.<br />
The vehicle type determines the type of parking space available for booking.

Additional vehicle types can be added. To do this, [contact us](https://www.surfy.pro/en-gb/contact).

### Associate vehicles with collaborators

For a collaborator to book a parking space, they must be associated with a vehicle.<br />
This vehicle is itself associated with a vehicle type and its license plate if necessary.<br />
It can be declared as belonging to the company.

To associate a vehicle with a collaborator, from the left menu,

-   type the collaborator's name in the search bar
-   click on the collaborator's name to view their record
-   click in the context menu (grey box below the search magnifying glass) to "Modify person XXX"
-   below the person's information, click on "Vehicle assignments" then "Create a vehicle assignment"
-   choose the vehicle to associate with the collaborator (generic or based on license plate)
-   validate creation

The vehicle is added below the creation module; the collaborator is associated with a vehicle.

### Bulk association of vehicles with collaborators

Vehicle associations with collaborators can be imported from an Excel file.
To do this, [contact us](https://www.surfy.pro/en-gb/contact)

### Building assignment for parking booking

For a collaborator to book a parking space, it is also necessary to assign the collaborator to a building with bookable parking spaces, and grant them access to booking.

To do this,

-   type the collaborator's name in the search bar
-   click on the collaborator's name to view their record
-   click in the context menu (grey box below the search magnifying glass) to "Modify person XXX"
-   below the person's information, click on "Building assignments"

-   if the person is already assigned to a building, check the "Allow parking booking in the building" box

-   if the person is not already assigned to a building, click on "Create a building assignment"
-   enter the building to assign
-   check the "Allow parking booking in the building" box
-   validate creation

The building is added below the creation module; the collaborator is assigned to this building.

When connecting to the Surfy platform, the user whose login email address matches that of the collaborator listed in Surfy and correctly configured will be able to book a parking space through the booking menu.

### Bulk assignment of collaborators to a building

Collaborator assignments to buildings can be imported from an Excel file.

To do this, see [Assign people to a building by Excel import](/en/docs/tutorials/affectations/persontobuilding/create#assign-people-to-the-building-by-excel-import) or [contact us](https://www.surfy.pro/en-gb/contact)
