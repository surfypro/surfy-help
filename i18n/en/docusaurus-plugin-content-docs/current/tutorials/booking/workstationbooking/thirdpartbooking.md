# Book on behalf of someone else

Surfy lets you book workstations for third parties (guests, executives, auditors, ...).

This feature requires the "Book on behalf of others" role to be added to the user, who can then book for other people.<br />
See the chapter [Assign a user role](/en/docs/access/intro#assign-a-user-role).<br />

Once the role has been assigned, the user can access the feature.

To book on behalf of someone else, from the left menu,

-   click on "Planning", "People's work location", then "Manage planning and make bookings for another person".
-   search for the person for whom you need to make a booking.

## If the person is in the Surfy database

-   click on their name to display their booking districts, if any.
-   book for the person as you would for yourself.

### If the person is not assigned to the desired district

-   click on one of the person's "unassigned buildings" to force their assignment to that building.
-   book for the person as you would for yourself.

## If the person is not in the Surfy database

-   create the person with their first name, last name, and email address (see [Create a person](/en/docs/tutorials/person/create)).
-   refresh the page (F5).
-   click on "Planning", "People's work location", then "Manage planning and make bookings for another person".
-   search for the newly created person for whom you need to make a booking.
-   click on one of the person's "unassigned buildings" to force their assignment to that building.
-   book for the person as you would for yourself.

:::tip GOOD TO KNOW
Assignment to a district or building remains in place for future bookings.<br />
To remove assignment to a district or building, see [Delete a district assignment](/en/docs/tutorials/affectations/dimensiontoperson/delete) or [building assignment](/en/docs/tutorials/affectations/persontobuilding/delete).
:::
