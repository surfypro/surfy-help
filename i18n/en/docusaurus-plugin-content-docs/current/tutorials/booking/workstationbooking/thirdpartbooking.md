# Book on behalf of someone else

Surfy lets you book workstations for third parties (collaborators outside your scope, executives, external guests, ...).

This feature requires the "Book on behalf of others" role to be added to the user, who can then book for other people.<br />
See the chapter [Assign a user role](/en/docs/access/intro#assign-a-user-role).<br />

Once the role has been assigned, the user can access the feature.

## Book for a collaborator

To book on behalf of a collaborator who is in the Surfy database, from the left menu,

-   click on "Planning", "People's work location", then "Manage planning and make bookings for another person".
-   search for the person for whom you need to make a booking.
-   click on their name to display their booking districts, if any.
-   book for the person as you would for yourself.

### If the person is not assigned to the desired district

-   click on one of the person's "unassigned buildings" to force their assignment to that building.
-   book for the person as you would for yourself.

:::tip GOOD TO KNOW
Assignment to a district or building remains in place for future bookings.<br />
To remove assignment to a district or building, see [Delete a district assignment](/en/docs/tutorials/affectations/dimensiontoperson/delete) or [building assignment](/en/docs/tutorials/affectations/persontobuilding/delete).
:::

## Book for an external guest

To temporarily book a workstation for an external guest, from the left menu,

-   click on "Planning", "People's work location", then "Create external guests, assign them, and manage their bookings".
-   if the person is not listed among the guests, click on "Create a guest".
-   enter their first name, last name, and email address.
-   enter the guest's period of presence.
-   click on "Create guest".

The invited person is added to the guest list.

To book a workstation for the guest,

-   click on the guest's name in the guest list.
-   assign the guest to the desired building.
-   book for the guest as you would for yourself.

To delete a guest,

-   click the "trash" icon at the end of the guest's row in the guest list.

:::tip GOOD TO KNOW
The guest will be automatically deleted at the end of their period of presence. They will therefore no longer be assigned to the building designated during the booking.
:::
