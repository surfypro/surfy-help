# About tutorials

Need a little help? Our tutorials will guide you through getting started with Surfy's features.

## [Surfaces in Surfy](/en/docs/surfaces)

To use Surfy, I need static 2D plans that will serve as background plans for each floor to build my spaces.<br />
In Surfy, I create a building then a floor with its background plan and scale so I can draw and qualify spaces.
I then get surface information by space typology or by any other custom layer: organizations, floor types, evacuation zones, cost centers, etc.<br />
I can also duplicate an existing building or floor to propose a new layout of my spaces and decide with full knowledge of my data.<br />

## [People in Surfy](/en/docs/people)

The list of occupants with additional information can be integrated into Surfy from an Excel file, via API with the HRIS, or manually as collaborators arrive.<br />
Easily find a collaborator's workstation or district, automatically visualize on plans the workstations of local security team members, structure and list teams, assign an ergonomic chair or a locker,... so many features explained here.<br />
Team moves are easily managed in Surfy using a simple and effective transfer matrix.<br />

## [Furniture in Surfy](/en/docs/objects)

Create and duplicate furniture in Surfy, to scale or as icons, from the object library or by creating your own object types or workstation types.<br />
Reference and locate your equipment on plans, know your inventory in all circumstances,<br />
Evaluate your capacity to receive visitors by locating free workstations and the number of available seats in the building.<br />

## [Booking with Surfy](/en/docs/booking)

With Surfy, the administrator has everything needed to easily set up booking for workstations, meeting spaces, or parking!<br />
Discover how to manage assignment districts, assign collaborators to them, and grant them booking access rights.<br />

The collaborator experience:
Book a workstation in the district assigned to you,<br />
Locate a meeting space by capacity, equipment, and building, and book your room directly in Outlook.<br />
Book a parking space according to your vehicle type.
