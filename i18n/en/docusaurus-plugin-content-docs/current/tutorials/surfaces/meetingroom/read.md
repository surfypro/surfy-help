# View meeting spaces

To view meeting spaces,

-   click on the "View and navigate in meeting rooms" icon at the top left of the screen under the company logo

Meeting spaces are grouped and displayed according to their representation on the plan, accompanied by the following information:

-   the space name
-   its capacity
-   its floor and building
-   its typology
-   its equipment

You can search for a meeting space by filtering by building, floor, capacity, or space typology to find the appropriate space and its geolocation.

The furniture present in the space is represented identically to the plans. Passing the mouse over the furniture displays the object type of each item in the tooltip.

Equipment (example: "Service equipment") is listed under the representation of each meeting space.

Clicking on the "road map" icon allows you to "locate the space on the plan" and displays the space in its context on the general floor plan.

Surfy lets you book available rooms displayed for viewing directly in Surfy via Outlook.

More information on [viewing and booking meeting rooms](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/meetingroombooking/meetingroom).
