# Surfy for Booking

## Book a workstation

You can book a workstation directly in Surfy or via the Teams application, for a full day or half-day.<br />
To access a booking district (or building), you must first be assigned to that district (or building).<br />
You can then book a workstation or declare remote work.<br />

[Configure Surfy for booking](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/workstationbooking/intro)<br />
[How to book a workstation](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/workstationbooking/workplace)<br />
[Teams booking user guide](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/workstationbooking/teams)<br />

## Book on behalf of someone else

Users with the "Book on behalf of others" role can book for one or more third parties, on the bookable building of their choice.

More information on [booking on behalf of someone else](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/workstationbooking/thirdpartbooking)

## Book a meeting space

Surfy provides easy access to all meeting spaces on your platform by location, capacity, or available equipment.

Bookable spaces can then be reserved directly in Surfy, and bookings are automatically integrated with Outlook.

More information on [meeting room administration](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/meetingroombooking/admin)

More information on [viewing and booking meeting rooms](/en/docs/tutorials/booking/meetingroombooking/meetingroom)

## Book a parking space

You can book a parking space in Surfy after your platform has been configured.

Each parking booking user is associated with a vehicle type (internal combustion, electric, hybrid) and a license plate number.
